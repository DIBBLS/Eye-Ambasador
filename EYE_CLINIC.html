What We Do

Clinical Services

Expert eye care from a registered optometrist — using professional instruments for accurate diagnosis and management.

Comprehensive Eye Exam

Full assessment of your visual health using ophthalmoscope and retinoscope. We check refractive errors, eye diseases, and overall eye health.

Refraction Test

Precise measurement of your prescription for corrective lenses — glasses or contact lenses for distance, reading, or progressive wear.

Glaucoma Management

Early detection and ongoing management of glaucoma — one of the leading causes of preventable blindness in Nigeria.

Cataract Screening

Clinical assessment for early signs of cataract formation. Timely detection enables better outcomes and appropriate referral.

Contact Lens Fitting

Professional fitting and supply of contact lenses. First-time wearers guided through fitting and care from start to finish.

Did You Know?

Dr. Aina believes an informed patient is a healthier patient. Here are a few things most people get wrong about their eyes.

Vision Myth

"Sitting too close to the TV ruins your eyesight."

Actually, there is no scientific evidence that sitting close to a screen causes permanent eye damage. It may cause temporary eye strain, but not lasting harm.

Did You Know

Glaucoma has no symptoms in its early stages.

Most people with glaucoma feel no pain and notice no vision changes until significant damage has occurred. Regular eye exams are your only protection.

Book a Check →
Eye Health

Your eyes can show signs of diabetes before your doctor does.

Diabetic retinopathy is often detected during a routine eye exam. A comprehensive eye test can reveal systemic conditions you may not know you have.
